# Organizations

Organizations on Opentribe represent teams, DAOs, projects, and companies that post opportunities for builders in the Polkadot ecosystem.

## Overview

Organizations can:

- **Post Opportunities** - Create bounties, grants, and RFPs
- **Manage Teams** - Invite members with different roles
- **Review Submissions** - Evaluate work and select winners
- **Track Payments** - Record and verify on-chain payments
- **Build Reputation** - Establish trust with the builder community

<Callout type="info">
  Each user can create up to 5 organizations. Organizations can have unlimited members with different permission levels.
</Callout>

## Creating an Organization

<Steps>

<Step title="Navigate to Organizations">
Go to Dashboard → Organizations and click "Create Organization"
</Step>

<Step title="Fill in Details">
Provide your organization information:
- **Name**: Your organization's display name
- **Handle**: Unique identifier (e.g., @moonbeam)
- **Description**: What your organization does
- **Logo**: Upload your logo (PNG, JPG, SVG)
- **Website**: Your main website URL
</Step>

<Step title="Add Social Links">
Connect your social presence:
- Twitter/X
- Discord
- Telegram
- GitHub
</Step>

<Step title="Configure Settings">
Set up your organization preferences:
- Default payment tokens
- Notification settings
- Public/private visibility
</Step>

</Steps>

## Team Roles & Permissions

Organizations have three role levels:

### Owner
- Full organization control
- Can delete organization
- Can transfer ownership
- All admin permissions

### Admin
- Post and manage opportunities
- Invite/remove members
- Review submissions
- Record payments
- Edit organization settings
- Cannot delete org or change owner

### Member
- Post opportunities
- Review submissions
- Comment on submissions
- View organization analytics
- Cannot manage team or settings

<Callout type="warning">
  Only organization owners can delete an organization or transfer ownership. Choose your initial owner carefully.
</Callout>

## Managing Your Team

### Inviting Members

<Tabs>
<Tab value="email" label="By Email">

1. Go to Organization Settings → Team
2. Click "Invite Member"
3. Enter email address
4. Select role (Admin or Member)
5. Add optional message
6. Send invitation

The invitee will receive an email with a link to join.

</Tab>

<Tab value="link" label="By Link">

1. Go to Organization Settings → Team
2. Click "Create Invite Link"
3. Select role for link
4. Set expiration (optional)
5. Share the link

Anyone with the link can join with the specified role.

</Tab>
</Tabs>

## Payment Management

Organizations handle payments directly:

1. **Select Winners** - Choose successful submissions
2. **Send Payment** - Use your wallet (external to Opentribe)
3. **Record Transaction** - Add transaction hash to Opentribe
4. **Automatic Verification** - Opentribe verifies on-chain

See our [payment guide](/docs/payments/making-payments) for details.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I change my organization's handle?
No, handles are permanent identifiers. Choose carefully when creating.

### How many organizations can I create?
Each user can create up to 5 organizations.

### Can I transfer an organization?
Yes, owners can transfer ownership to another member through settings.

### What happens if I delete an organization?
All associated data (opportunities, submissions) is permanently deleted. Active opportunities should be completed first.

### Can organizations be verified?
Yes, contact support for verification. This adds a badge and increases builder trust.
