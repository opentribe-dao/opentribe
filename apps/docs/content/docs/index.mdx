## How It Works

### Discover Opportunities

Browse bounties, grants, and RFPs from across the Polkadot ecosystem - all aggregated in one platform.

### Submit Your Work

Apply for grants or submit your work to bounties. Track your submissions in your profile (builders) or dashboard (organizations).

### Get Paid

Organizations send payments directly to your Polkadot wallet. Opentribe verifies and tracks all payments on-chain for transparency.
