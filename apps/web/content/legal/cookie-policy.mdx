## Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, which ones we use, and how you can manage your preferences.

## Cookies we use

- Necessary cookies: required for core functionality (security, authentication, preferences essential to the service).
- Preference cookies: remember choices like language and display settings.
- Analytics cookies: help us understand usage so we can improve the site.

## Managing cookies

You can accept or reject non-essential cookies using the banner on our website. You can also control cookies via your browser settings, where you can remove or block cookies. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@opentribe.io.
